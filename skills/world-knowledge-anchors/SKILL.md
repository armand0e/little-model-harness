# World Knowledge Anchors

High-confidence reference facts to answer from and to check claims against. If a statement contradicts an anchor here, distrust the statement. For anything not here or at memory's edge, apply calibrated-uncertainty rather than guessing.

## Geography

- 7 continents; 5 oceans (Pacific largest & deepest, then Atlantic, Indian, Southern, Arctic). ~195 countries.
- Largest countries by area: Russia, Canada, US, China, Brazil. By population: India, China (~1.4B each), then US, Indonesia, Pakistan.
- Longest rivers: Nile & Amazon (~6,600 km, contested). Largest by volume: Amazon. Tallest mountain: Everest 8,849 m. Largest desert: Antarctica (cold desert), then Sahara (hot). Largest lake: Caspian Sea. Largest rainforest: Amazon.
- Earth: ~40,000 km circumference, radius 6,371 km, one Sun at ~150M km. Tilted 23.5° → seasons (opposite in N/S hemispheres). 24 h rotation, ~365.25 day orbit.
- Continents/capitals to anchor: US–Washington DC, UK–London, France–Paris, Germany–Berlin, Russia–Moscow, China–Beijing, Japan–Tokyo, India–New Delhi, Brazil–Brasília, Canada–Ottawa, Australia–Canberra (not Sydney), Egypt–Cairo. (Many capitals aren't the biggest city — Canberra, Brasília, Ottawa, Washington, Ankara.)

## History timeline (anchors, CE unless noted)

- Agriculture ~10,000 BCE. Writing ~3200 BCE (Sumer). Egyptian pyramids ~2600 BCE.
- Classical: Greek golden age ~5th c. BCE; Roman Empire ~27 BCE–476 CE (west). 
- ~1450 Gutenberg press. 1492 Columbus reaches Americas. 1500s Reformation.
- 1600s–1700s Scientific Revolution & Enlightenment. 1776 US independence; 1789 French Revolution.
- ~1760–1840 Industrial Revolution. 1861–65 US Civil War.
- WWI 1914–1918. 1929 Great Depression. WWII 1939–1945 (US enters 1941, ends 1945). 
- 1945 UN & nuclear age. 1947 India independence. 1969 Moon landing. 1989 Berlin Wall falls; ~1991 USSR dissolves. 1991 Web goes public. 2007 iPhone.
- Order-of-magnitude checks: printing press before the American Revolution; Rome before the Renaissance; WWI before WWII. Use these to reject garbled chronologies.

## Science constants & scales

- Speed of light 3×10⁸ m/s. g ≈ 9.8 m/s². Absolute zero −273.15°C. Water freezes 0°C / boils 100°C (sea level).
- Atom sizes ~0.1 nm; visible light ~400–700 nm; human hair ~100 µm; cell ~10 µm. 
- Age of universe ~13.8 billion yr; Earth ~4.5 billion yr; life ~3.8 billion yr; dinosaurs extinct ~66 million yr ago; Homo sapiens ~300,000 yr.
- Human body: ~37°C, ~37 trillion cells, ~60% water, ~206 bones (adult), 23 chromosome pairs, DNA ~3 billion base pairs.
- Periodic table: H lightest, ~118 elements; most common in universe H then He; in Earth's crust O then Si; in the body O, C, H, N.
- Solar system order: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune (Pluto reclassified dwarf 2006). Jupiter largest planet; Sun holds ~99.8% of system mass.

## Human body & biology basics

- Cells → tissues → organs → systems. DNA→RNA→protein (central dogma). Evolution by natural selection (Darwin). 
- Blood carries O₂ (lungs) and nutrients; heart pumps ~100,000×/day. Brain ~86 billion neurons.
- Bacteria (no nucleus) vs viruses (not cells, need a host) vs your cells (eukaryotic). Antibiotics hit bacteria, not viruses.

## How to use these

These are anchors, not the whole map. Reason OUTWARD from them: an unknown country's population is checkable against "world is 8 billion"; an unknown event's date against the timeline above; a physics claim against the constants. When a question needs a fact NOT anchored here and you're unsure, say so and give a bounded estimate — never fabricate a specific date, statistic, or name to seem complete (see calibrated-uncertainty).
