# Biology Essentials

## Cells & molecules of life

- All life is cells: bacteria are single cells without a nucleus (prokaryotes); plants/animals/fungi have nucleated cells (eukaryotes). A human is ~30–40 trillion cells, plus a comparable number of resident bacteria (mostly gut — mostly beneficial).
- **DNA** is the instruction book: same DNA in nearly every cell of a body; genes are recipes for proteins; proteins do the work (enzymes, structure, signaling). Flow: DNA → RNA → protein. Human genome ≈ 3 billion base pairs, ~20,000 protein-coding genes. Humans share ~99.9% of DNA with each other, ~98%+ with chimps.
- Energy: mitochondria burn glucose + O₂ → ATP + CO₂ + H₂O (why you inhale O₂ and exhale CO₂). Plants run photosynthesis: CO₂ + H₂O + light → glucose + O₂ (they make food from air and light; soil supplies water and minerals, not the carbon — a tree's mass comes mostly from CO₂).

## Genetics quick kit

- Two copies of each gene (one per parent). Dominant allele masks recessive: brown-eyed parents CAN have a blue-eyed child (both carriers, 1/4 chance); two blue-eyed parents very rarely produce brown (eye color is actually polygenic — most traits are many-gene + environment, not single-gene).
- Sex: XX female, XY male; the sperm's chromosome decides. X-linked recessive conditions (color blindness, hemophilia) hit males more (one X, no backup).
- Heritable = influenced by genes, NOT "fixed at birth"; heritability is a population statistic, not a personal destiny.

## Evolution (the actual theory, not the caricature)

- Variation (mutation, recombination) + differential reproduction (selection) + inheritance → populations change. Individuals don't evolve; populations do, across generations.
- "Survival of the fittest" = best-REPRODUCING in that environment, not strongest. Evolution has no goals, no ladder; humans didn't evolve "from chimps" — we share a common ancestor (~6–7 Mya).
- It's observable in real time: **antibiotic resistance** is evolution happening in hospitals. Which is why: finish the course, don't demand antibiotics for viruses.
- Timescales: life ~3.8 billion yr; animals ~600 Myr; dinosaurs died 66 Mya; Homo sapiens ~300,000 yr; agriculture ~12,000 yr. Humans and (non-avian) dinosaurs never coexisted — birds ARE surviving dinosaurs.

## Human body numbers

Heart ~60–100 bpm, ~5 L blood, pumps ~5 L/min. Lungs ~12–20 breaths/min. Body temp 37°C (fever ≥ 38). Blood pressure ~120/80. Kidneys filter ~180 L/day (reabsorbing almost all). Brain ~1.4 kg, ~2% of mass, ~20% of energy. Nerve signals up to ~120 m/s (not light speed). Skin = largest organ. Stomach acid pH ~1.5. You use 100% of your brain (the 10% claim is a myth). Adult: 206 bones, ~600 muscles.
